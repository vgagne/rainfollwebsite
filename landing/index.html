Up and Running in 3 Steps

No plumber. No landlord permission needed.

01 Step 1

Twist into place

Extend the tension bar between your shower walls (fits 56–80 inch wide showers) and twist until snug. Zero drilling, zero damage.

02 Step 2

Close the sheaths

Slide the sheath close and lock with the pins. Move the rainfall head wherever you want — left, center, or right.

03 Step 3

Connect to your plumbing

Connect the Handheld shower head to your existing plumbing. Then connect the hoses. That's it.
