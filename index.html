Luxury in Minutes

No tools. No drilling. Just pure indulgence.

01
Step 1

Twist Tension Bar Into Place

Extend the tension bar between your shower walls (works with spaces 56-80 inches wide) and twist until firmly secured. No drilling required.

02
Step 2

Close Safety Sheaths

Slide the safety sheaths into position to lock the system securely in place. This ensures everything stays stable during use.

03
Step 3

Connect Shower Hose

Attach the hose to your existing plumbing—hand tight, no tools needed. Turn on the water and start enjoying your new luxury rainfall shower.

Works in showers with opposing walls 56–80 inches apart
